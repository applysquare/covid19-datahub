Term 2 starts on the 1st of June 2020, and you can enrol in Term 2 up until 28 June 2020. Given the uncertainty with travel restrictions, you may want to think about starting in Term 3. Term 3 enrolment begins as early as 3 August 2020, right up until 11 October 2020.
