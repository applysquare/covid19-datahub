On April 1, University Health Services announced updates to the Student Health Plan regarding referrals, coverage for COVID-19-related care, and coverage for telehealth visits. The changes are intended to make it easier for individuals on the Student Health Plan to receive services during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The changes apply to undergraduate students, graduate students and student dependents enrolled in the Student Health Plan for the 2019-20 plan year.
